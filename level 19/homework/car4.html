BMW

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) is a well-known German manufacturer of luxury cars and motorcycles,
founded in 1916 and headquartered in Munich. The brand is famous for its combination of performance, innovation,
and high-quality engineering. BMW produces a wide range of vehicles, including sedans, SUVs, and electric models,
with popular cars like the 3 Series, 5 Series, X5, and the electric i4 and iX.

With the slogan "The Ultimate Driving Machine," BMW emphasizes a sporty and premium driving experience.
The company is also the parent of MINI and Rolls-Royce Motor Cars, further strengthening its presence in
the luxury automotive market. Over the years, BMW has built a strong reputation for
combining driving pleasure with cutting-edge technology and design.
