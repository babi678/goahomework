Ferrari

Ferrari is a legendary Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939 and officially established as
an independent company in 1947. Known for its iconic prancing horse logo, Ferrari is synonymous with speed, performance, and exclusivity.
The company is headquartered in Maranello, Italy, and has a long history in motorsports, especially Formula One,
where it is the most successful team in history. Ferrari cars are famous for their sleek design, powerful engines,
and cutting-edge technology, making them a symbol of status and engineering excellence. Models like the Ferrari 488,
LaFerrari, and SF90 Stradale showcase the brand's commitment to innovation while maintaining its racing heritage.
