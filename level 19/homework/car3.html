Mercedes

Mercedes-Benz is a world-renowned German automotive brand known for producing luxury vehicles,
buses, and trucks. Founded in 1926 through the merger of Karl Benz's and Gottlieb Daimler's companies,
Mercedes is considered one of the oldest and most respected car manufacturers in the world.
Headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany, the brand is known for its elegance, advanced engineering,
and innovation in safety and performance. Mercedes offers a wide range of vehicles,
from luxury sedans and SUVs to high-performance AMG models and cutting-edge electric cars under the EQ lineup.
The iconic three-pointed star logo symbolizes the brand's dominance on land, sea, and air, reflecting its legacy of
excellence in automotive engineering.
