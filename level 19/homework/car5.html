Tesla

Tesla, Inc. is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company founded in 2003,
with Elon Musk as its most well-known figure and current CEO. Headquartered in Palo Alto,
California, Tesla is widely recognized for transforming the automotive industry by making
electric vehicles more popular, high-performing, and technologically advanced.
The company produces several electric models, including the Model S, Model 3, Model X, and Model Y,
as well as upcoming vehicles like the Cybertruck, Tesla Semi, and a new Roadster. In addition to cars,
Tesla develops clean energy products such as solar panels, the Solar Roof, and home battery systems like the Powerwall.
Known for its innovation, software-driven features, and focus on sustainability, Tesla continues to lead the global
shift toward electric mobility and renewable energy solutions.
